# MASTER PLAN: [SIC] MENTAL_LABORATORY v3.0 // THE ESOTERIC VOID

**Project:** [SIC] Website Expansion
**Module:** The Laboratory / `laboratory.html`
**Concept:** "A digital liminal space representing the subconscious conflict of the band."

## Overview
This document outlines the architectural and creative vision for the "Mental Laboratory". The goal is to move beyond a standard "website" into an interactive art installation that users experience through their browser.

## Core Pillars
1.  **Indeterminacy**: Nothing should feel static. Text moves, images glitch, sounds shift.
2.  **The Viewer is the Subject**: Through the webcam mirror and direct address, the user is part of the experiment.
3.  **Hacking/Esotericism**: The aesthetics of "forbidden knowledge", number stations, and intercepted signals.

## Phased Execution Roadmap

### [PHASE 1: THE CONTAINER] - Visuals & Physics
*   **Goal**: Establish the environment.
*   **Tech**: Three.js / Matter.js.
*   **Key Feature**: "The Idea Collider" (Gravity-based word physics).
*   **Atmosphere**: CRT Scanlines, Chromatic Aberration, Liquid Distortion shader on cursor.

### [PHASE 2: THE ETHER] - Audio & Tuning
*   **Goal**: Sonify the void.
*   **Tech**: WebAudio API.
*   **Key Feature**: "The Frequency Tuner" (Dial to find signals in noise).
*   **Content**: White noise generators, hidden track reverses.

### [PHASE 3: THE SUBCONSCIOUS] - Logic & Text
*   **Goal**: Communicate with the machine.
*   **Tech**: Vanilla JS text processing / Arrays.
*   **Key Features**: 
    *   "The Oracle" (Cryptic answering machine).
    *   "Subliminal Injection" (Flash frames).

### [PHASE 4: THE MIRROR] - Hardware Integration
*   **Goal**: Break the fourth wall.
*   **Tech**: `navigator.mediaDevices.getUserMedia`.
*   **Key Feature**: "The Mirror of Duality" (High-contrast webcam feed).
